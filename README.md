# Capy's Happy List
Capy's Happy List is a to-do app with a twist — every task you complete makes your little capybara companion happier. Add your tasks, check them off one by one, and watch Capy's mood transform from a bored, fist-on-chin grump all the way to a peace-sign-throwing, tongue-out party animal. A happiness meter tracks your progress, and Capy's face animates through 5 distinct moods as you go. Finish everything on your list and he absolutely loses it - confetti and all. Built in a cozy butter-yellow gingham and matcha green theme, it makes even your most dreaded to-do list feel like a good time.
